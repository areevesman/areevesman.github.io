About me

I am a data scientist who excels at machine learning, exploratory analysis, and data visualization.

I hold a bachelor's degree in mathematics from the University of California, Santa Barbara and a Master's degree in Data Science from the University of San Francisco.

Background

As a Data Engineering Rookie at at Under Armour, I analysed runner route popularity and designed algorithms to identify changes in user activity from GPS time series data.

Before joining Under Armour, I worked with the National Center for Ecological Analysis and Synthesis, where I contributed to open source R packages for managing submissions to the Arctic Data Center. In addition, I used R and Python to clean and aggregate data from National Science Foundation funded research projects to prepare them for publication.

My interests

In my free time, I enjoy learning new skills by working on personal projects.
